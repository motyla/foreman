About

GNI is aimed to be a single address for all machines life cycle management.

GNI integrates with Puppet (and acts as web front end to it).
GNI would take care for the bare bone provisioning until the point puppet is running, letting Puppet do what it does best.

Gini will show you Systems inventory (based on facter) and can provide information about hosts based on Puppet reports.

GNI can create everything you need when adding a new machine into your network, its goal would be to manage automatically everything you would normally do manually - that could include DNS, DHCP, TFTP, PuppetCA, CMDB and everything else you might consider useful.
The idea is you can always rebuilt your machines from scratch

GNI is design to work in a Large enterprise, where multiple domains, subnets and puppetmasters are required. in many cases, GNI could help remote provisions when no experienced technicians are available.

Features

  • Browse your puppet hosts inventory(facts)
  • Provision your servers (currently supported Kickstart, Jumpstart and preseed)
  • Puppet external nodes support
  • PuppetCA management
  • Import Pupppet facts (if not using storeconfigs)
